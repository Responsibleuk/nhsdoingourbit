Welcome to Doing Our Bit

By The NHS for The NHS

DoingOurBit is a free fitness platform offering a wide range of bespoke workouts gifted by carefully selected PTs and fitness instructors. The workouts are accessible, friendly and welcoming with plenty for beginners. Devised in lockdown, this genuine and heartfelt gift to the hard-working NHS community is set to stay long into the future.

About Doing Our Bit

#DoingOurBit is purposefully simple to use and carefully presented by the PTs so that it’s immediately accessible to all – especially for inactive people wanting to take their first steps into activity.

While it started out to help NHS staff through the worst of the Covid-19 pandemic, #DoingOurBit has plans to grow, improve and remain fit for purpose for the NHS long into the future. Its success relies on contributions from qualified PTs, support from fitness industry experts and bodies, awareness generated by media and on-going engagement from NHS wellness leaders.
